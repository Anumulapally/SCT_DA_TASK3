# SCT_DA_TASK3

📌 Task Description

Create an interactive dashboard using Power BI on the HR Employee Attrition dataset to analyze why employees are leaving the organization.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop
HR Employee Attrition Dataset (IBM)

📂 Dataset

IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
1,470 employees
35 columns

✅ Visuals Created

Employee Count KPI Card (1470)
Attrition by Job Role (Bar Chart)
Attrition by Age (Column Chart)
Attrition by Department (Bar Chart)
Average Monthly Income by Attrition (Column Chart)
Slicers for Gender, Department and Job Role

💡 Key Insights

Sales Executives have the highest attrition count
Research & Development has the most employees leaving
Employees aged 28-35 leave the most
Employees who left earn ~$4K vs ~$6K for those who stayed
Human Resources department has the lowest attrition
